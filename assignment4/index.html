Crystals Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to teh random number. You lose the game if your total score exceeds the value of the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden frm you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the both the value of the random number and the values of each crystal will change.
