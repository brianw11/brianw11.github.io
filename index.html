About Me



Making all those little fluffies that live in the clouds. Automatically, all of these beautiful, beautiful things will happen. I'll go over the colors one more time that we use: Titanium white, Thalo green, Prussian blue, Van Dyke brown, Alizarin crimson, Sap green, Cad yellow, and Permanent red.


So often we avoid running water, and running water is a lot of fun. We'll play with clouds today. Painting should do one thing. It should put happiness in your heart. There it is. That's the way I look when I get home late; black and blue. There comes a nice little fluffer.


Let's put some happy little bushes on the other side now. There is immense joy in just watching - watching all the little creatures in nature. We tell people sometimes: we're like drug dealers, come into town and get everybody absolutely addicted to painting. It doesn't take much to get you addicted. Maybe there's a happy little Evergreen that lives here. In painting, you have unlimited power. You have the ability to move mountains. You can bend rivers. But when I get home, the only thing I have power over is the garbage.


Nothing's gonna make your husband or wife madder than coming home and having a snow-covered dinner. I was blessed with a very steady hand; and it comes in very handy when you're doing these little delicate things. Do an almighty painting with us.
